📖 How to Play

Getting Started

You start with your bare hands in an infinite world. Gather resources, craft tools, and build structures!

Mining

Left-click on blocks to break them:

  • Bare Hand: Breaks wood slowly, stone very slowly
  • Axe: 1.25x faster on wood (60 uses)
  • Pickaxe: Much faster on stone (50 uses)
  • Knife: Utility tool (40 uses)

Crafting

Press I to open inventory. Place items in the grid and click the result to craft!

Placing Blocks

Select an item with 1-9 and right-click to place it in the world.

Smelting

Craft a Forge (5 stone blocks), right-click it to open. Add fuel (wood) and items to smelt!

Saving

Progress auto-saves to your browser. Manually save from menu to use save slots.

📚 Crafting Recipes

Basic Resources

  • Planks: 1 Wood → 4 Planks
  • Sticks: 2 Planks (vertical) → 4 Sticks

Tools

  • Axe: 2 Planks + 2 Sticks (in T shape)
  • Pickaxe: 3 Planks (top) + 2 Sticks (vertical)
  • Knife: 2 Planks + 1 Stick (vertical)

Structures

  • Crafting Table: 4 Planks (2x2)
  • Forge: 5 Stone (cross pattern)

Biomes

  • 🌲 Green Forest
  • 🔴 Red Forest
  • 🍂 Mixed Forest
  • 🍁 Autumn Grove
  • 🏜️ Desert
  • 🌊 Ocean
